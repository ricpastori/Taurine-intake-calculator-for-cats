Taurine intake calculator

This calculator is designed for healthy adult cats and provides an approximate estimate of their daily taurine requirement.

Source: Earle, K. E., & Smith, P. M. (1991). The effect of dietary taurine content on the plasma taurine concentration of the cat. British Journal of Nutrition, 66(2), 227-235.

N.B. These results are only for guidance. It's always a good idea to check with a trusted veterinarian to make sure your cat is happy and healthy.
